Welcome to Saar, an independent podcast channel illuminating the authentic, untold stories of Afghanistan. “Saar” means “Starling” in Persian, a bird known for its communal nature and harmonious voice. Mirroring this, Saar serves as a collective voice, channeling the messages of Afghan individuals living through current challenges.

These individuals, choosing to remain anonymous, send us letters detailing their lived experiences, enabling the world to gain insights into the reality of the Afghan people. Saar is determined in its mission to support their fight for human rights and raise awareness of their ongoing struggles. Join us, as we bring these untold stories to light, standing in solidarity with the Afghan community.

About Saar
